PROJECT: FANHUB – GLOBAL FOOTBALL FAN PLATFORM

Design Direction:
Create a premium football experience inspired by a fresh green stadium on match day.

Visual Identity:

* Main Theme: Fresh Stadium Green
* Style: Modern, energetic, immersive, premium
* Feel: Standing inside a world-class football stadium before kickoff
* Atmosphere: Bright daylight, lush green grass, packed crowd, subtle stadium lights

Color Palette:

* Primary Green: #1DB954
* Stadium Grass: #22C55E
* Dark Turf: #166534
* Pitch Lines: #FFFFFF
* Background: #071A0F
* Accent Gold: #FACC15
* Card Surface: rgba(255,255,255,0.08)

Background Design:

* Full-screen football stadium backdrop
* Fresh green pitch visible in lower section
* Stadium seating blurred in background
* Soft sunlight beams across the field
* Subtle crowd atmosphere
* Modern glassmorphism overlays

Homepage Hero:

* Large stadium banner image
* Live football atmosphere
* Dynamic welcome message:
  "The Home of Global Football Fans"

Navigation:

* Home
* Fixtures

* Nation Communities
* Leaderboards
* Match Center
* Profile

UI Components:

* Rounded modern cards
* Frosted glass effect
* Floating action buttons
* Smooth animations
* Stadium-inspired gradients
* Green glowing accents

Match Cards:

* Team flags

* Stadium venue

Fan Community:

* Nation-specific fan hubs
* Match discussions

* Reputation points

Special Effects:

* Stadium floodlight glow
* Animated grass texture
* Smooth page transitions
* Goal celebration animation

Typography:

* Bold sports-style headings

Mobile Experience:

* Fast loading
* PWA ready

Overall Goal:
Make users feel like they are inside a vibrant football stadium every time they open FanHub. The design should be fresh, exciting, premium, and focused on the beauty of the green football pitch and global fan culture.
